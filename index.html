Welcome! My name is Carmen Yuchen Guo.

I am a graduate student in Computational Social Science at UC San Diego.

carmenk1210 [at] gmail [dot] com · LinkedIn · GitHub

I work at the intersection of computational social science, data analytics, and machine learning, with a particular interest in applying statistical and computational methods to understand social and political phenomena, and to drive data-driven decision making.

Previously worked in communication consultancy and higher education management, I bring a practical perspective to data science, focusing on translating data into actionable insights. My broader interests include data science for institutional research, public opinion analysis and AI for good.

I am currently pursuing my masters degree in Computational Social Science at University of California, San Diego. Before that, I obtained my M.S. in Marketing from The University of Hong Kong, and my B.A. in Advertising from Peking University.

Beyond research and professional work, I'm passionate about tennis, cinema, and photography.
